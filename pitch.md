## 🚨 Slide 2: The Problem
### The Sustainability Gap
1. **The Awareness Barrier**: 85% of people want to live more sustainably, but have no idea where their personal emissions come from.
2. **Action Paralysis**: Climate anxiety makes people feel powerless. Generic advice like "consume less water" fails to inspire permanent change.
3. **Tracking Friction**: Manually logging electricity bills, fuel receipts, and daily diets is tedious. People give up within days.

---

## 💡 Slide 3: The Solution (EcoTrack AI)
### Building Eco-Habits, Gamified
EcoTrack AI is a full-stack platform transforming carbon footprint accounting:
- **Instant AI Calculations**: A multi-step calculator that yields a personalized Carbon Score (1-100) and Rating (A-F).
- **Gemini AI Coaching**: Chat with an AI Sustainability Coach for customized carbon-reduction plans.
- **Computer Vision Receipts**: Avoid manual data entry. Upload utility bills or gasoline pump receipts; our Gemini-powered AI parses them instantly.
- **Missions & Rewards (XP)**: Record positive actions (cycling, carpooling, power saving) to earn Eco Points.
- **Reforestation Marketplace**: Convert virtual points into actual verified trees planted on our interactive simulator canvas.

---

## 📊 Slide 4: Hackathon Winning Features
### What Sets EcoTrack AI Apart?
- **AI Receipt Scanner**: Leverages Gemini Vision API to convert standard household paper receipts into concrete carbon emissions stats.
- **Voice-Based Coach Assistant**: Seamless text-to-speech integration. Users can listen to carbon saving recommendations rather than reading lengthy documents.
- **Tree Impact Simulator**: Drag-and-drop animated canvas showing how saplings scale up carbon absorption compared to smartphones charged or car mileage.
- **Offline Mock Fallback**: The app operates with complete interactivity using pre-seeded, simulated data, making it zero-friction for judges to inspect.

---

## 🛠️ Slide 5: Tech Stack & Architecture
### A Modern, Scalable Web Infrastructure
- **Frontend**: Next.js 15 App Router, TypeScript, Tailwind CSS, Framer Motion (smooth transition states), Recharts (data visualizations).
- **Backend**: Node.js & Express.js server providing REST APIs for dashboard stats, PDF compilation, and AI pipelines.
- **Database**: Prisma ORM, SQLite for immediate local execution (swappable to PostgreSQL in one config line).
- **AI Core**: Google Gemini SDK (`gemini-1.5-flash`) handling text and multi-modal bill analyses.
